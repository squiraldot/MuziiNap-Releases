## Features ✨️    
- Play music from YT Music or YouTube for free, without ads
- Steam in High quality for YT Music Premium users
- Browsing Home, Songs, Podcast, & Local Lib. on MuziiNap
- Search everything on MuziiNap what you wants...
- Login with YT Account and Sync your YT Music, also you can add musics from MuziiNap in your YT Albums
- Caching and offline playback support
- Synced lyrics from LRCLIB, and YouTube Transcript
- Personalization and you can add more than one YT Accounts
- SponsorBlock and Return YouTube Dislike's are Supported
- Android Auto with online content
- And many more!   

## Privacy
MuziiNap doesn't have any tracker or third-party server for collecting user data, only one key use your listening history record for giving better recommendations.
